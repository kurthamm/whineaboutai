Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2025

TL;DR: We collect minimal data, protect your privacy, and use cookies/analytics to improve your AI complaint experience. Your complaints may be displayed anonymously to help others suffer together.

1. Information We Collect

Complaint Data: We collect the AI complaints you submit through our platform. This data is used to display anonymous complaints, improve our service, and help others relate to your digital suffering.

Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors navigate our site. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Device type and browser information
  • General location (country/city level)
  • How you found our site (search engines, social media, etc.)
  • Interaction with our AI-powered features

Chat Data: Conversations with WhineBot may be logged to improve our AI therapist's responses and for debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Display anonymous complaints to create a community of shared AI frustration
  • Improve our website performance and user experience
  • Enhance our AI-powered features (complaint enhancement, predictions, etc.)
  • Generate aggregate statistics about AI complaint trends
  • Provide customer support when needed

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Google Analytics, Vercel (hosting), OpenAI (for AI features)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Anonymous Display: Your complaints may appear anonymously on our site

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics (website performance tracking)
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Enabling interactive features like voting and reactions
  • AdSense advertising (if applicable)
Cookie Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure hosting infrastructure via Vercel
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we have collected
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Age Restrictions

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Users

Our service is hosted in the United States. By using our site, international users consent to the transfer and processing of their data in the US.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
